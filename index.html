Oslob Whale Sharks

Oslob, Cebu is a famous destination for whale shark watching. The whale sharks in Oslob are considered as the living species of the 'largest fish in the world' and can reach up to 15 meters long

Kawasan Falls

Nestled in the heart of Cebu, Kawasan Falls is a pristine natural wonder that captivates visitors with its stunning beauty and serene ambiance. This multi-tiered waterfall, located in the municipality of Badian, is renowned for its crystal-clear turquoise waters, lush green surroundings, and tranquil atmosphere, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.
